Hey! I am Aman Bansal. I am a final year undergraduate in Computer Science and Engineering Department at IIT Bombay. I would be completing my degree (with Honours) in May 2020. I enjoy spending time pondering over tough problems, especially in the field of algorithms. As a result, I have been quite active in doing research as well as in the Competitive Programming world. The former requires perseverence while the latter, fast thinking, and I find myself learning the best of both worlds.

Industry Exprience: I have completed two industry internships, one as a Software Development Engineer at Rubrik which is a very fast growing cloud data management company headquartered in Silicon Valley and other as a Machine Learning Engineer at Haptik which is a leading Conversational AI startup out of India. From both of my internships, I learned a lot about the importance of writing industry level code, getting code reviewed, asking for help and helping out others, and not hesitating in raising your suggestions. I made a lot of friends and really enjoyed my time during the internships.

Research Exposure: I am currently doing my B.Tech. thesis with Prof. Supratik Chakraborty and Prof. Akshay in the field of Formal Methods and Verification. Indian Scientists' Response to CoViD-19



Indian Scientists’ Response to CoViD-19 (ISRC) is a voluntary group of over 200 concerned scientist citizens of India who have come together in this time of crisis to discuss the rapidly evolving situation with its dire need for science communication. Please visit our website to know more about our volunteering work which involves:

  • Trying to predict the pattern of coronavirus outbreak in India using the information available from other countries,
  • Looking at the information spreading in India through a scientific lens to classify it as either true or hoax,
  • Disseminating information which debunk the hoaxes circulating in the community through social media, and
  • Translating all the content in local indic langugages to expand the reach of legitimate information.
